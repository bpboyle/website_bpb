About Me

My primary research interests are: The relationship between electoral rules such as compulsory voting and voter registration, and levels of social inequality across political behaviour and political attitudes. As well as political communication, looking at political campaigning on twitter, and political representation in UK public broadcasting.

I completed my PhD at University of Strathclyde.
